Our Projects and Proposals

Past Projects

Project: NAARI PRAJWALAN - WOMENS NATURAL ROLE IS TO BE A PILLAR OF THE FAMILY

Creation of self-help groups.

Project: Operation RED ALERT with MY Choice Foundation

Awareness about women trafficking and child safety among students and all communities in the rural villages.

Project: Cleft Lip Operations under Operation SMILE

Under Operation SMILE, free operations were done to children with cleft lips.

Project: Mission Sunehrakal under ITC Limited in Orissa

Digging of ponds to store rainwater in Orissa.

Project: Waste Collection and Management Project for Recycling by SIBARD in Collaboration with Ramky Limited(rrrl)

Awareness on wet waste and dry waste, and the waste collected was recycled.

Project: Flood Relief Programs under AP State Social Welfare Board

Created Self Help groups of women to bring awareness about self hygiene and precautions about safety.

Project: Eradication of Child Labour under Rajiv Vidya Mission

Awareness on child labour and education in rural areas.

Project: Baseline Survey by SIBARD for District Water and Sanitation Committee

Survey conducted in Bayyaram regarding number of households, usage of toilets, water supply etc.
